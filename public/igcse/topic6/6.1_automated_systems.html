Apply It: Irrigation Control

How would sensors, a controller, and actuators keep crops watered automatically?

Sensors: soil moisture, weather feed; Controller: compares to thresholds; Actuators: pumps/valves.

Should We Automate?

Advantages
  • Speed, consistency, 24/7 operation
  • Safety: keeps humans away from hazards
  • Optimum control: small adjustments quickly
Disadvantages
  • High setup and maintenance cost
  • Cyber/logic risks; unforeseen states
  • Skill shift: oversight not manual control

Clarify

Question: Why keep a human supervisor if the system is automatic?

Answer: Edge cases, safety overrides, and accountability require human judgment.

Question 1

"Automated systems eliminate the need for any human involvement."

Answer: False

Humans still monitor, override, and maintain systems.

Question 2

"ADC is needed when sensors output analogue signals."

Answer: True

Analogue sensor data must be digitised for the microprocessor.

Question 3

"Automated systems always reduce costs immediately."

Answer: False

They often have high setup/maintenance costs; savings come over time.
