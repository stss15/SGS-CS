- **Type**: Comparison Visual
- **Description**: Contrasting 1D Barcodes and 2D QR Codes.
- **Prompt**: Create a split comparison diagram.
  - **Left Side (Barcode)**: Image of a standard barcode. Label: "1D Barcode (Linear)". Text: "Holds small amount of data (approx 30 digits). Read by laser/LED. Need direct line of sight. Used in supermarkets (Stock control)."
  - **Right Side (QR Code)**: Image of a QR code. Label: "2D QR Code (Matrix)". Text: "Holds large amount of data (approx 7000 digits). Read by cameras/smartphones. No direct line of sight needed. Used for URLs, tickets, advertising."

## Concept: Touch Screen Technologies
- **Type**: Comparison Table / Visual
- **Description**: Comparing Resistive, Capacitive, and Infrared screens.
- **Prompt**: Create a 3-column comparison visual for Touch Screens.
  - **Column 1: Resistive**: Visual of two layers pressing together. Text: "Two layers + Air gap. Pressure needed. Cheap. Works with gloves/stylus. No multi-touch. Poor visibility in sun."
  - **Column 2: Capacitive**: Visual of finger disrupting electric field. Text: "Glass + Conductive layer. Uses body electricity. Durable. Multi-touch. Good visibility. Bare finger only."
  - **Column 3: Infrared**: Visual of grid of beams breaking. Text: "LEDs and Sensors on edges. Beam break detection. Multi-touch. Durable. Can use gloves/stylus. Expensive."

## Concept: Inkjet vs Laser Printers
- **Type**: Comparison Infographic
- **Description**: Contrasting the two main printer types.
- **Prompt**: Create a specific comparison chart for "Inkjet vs Laser Printers".
  - **Inkjet**:
    - **Tech**: "Liquid Ink (Thermal bubble or Piezoelectric)."
    - **Pros**: "Cheap hardware. High quality photos."
    - **Cons**: "Expensive ink. Slow. Smudges."
    - **Use**: "Home use, Photos."
  - **Laser**:
    - **Tech**: "Powdered Toner + Static Electricity + Fuser."
    - **Pros**: "Fast. Cheap per page. High volume. Dry print."
    - **Cons**: "Expensive hardware. Bulky."
    - **Use**: "Offices, High volume text."
