# 10.3 Logic Circuits - Revision Artifact Prompts

## Concept: Building Logic Circuits
- **Type**: Process Diagram
- **Description**: How to combine gates.
- **Prompt**: Create a diagram for "Logic Circuit Construction".
  - **Inputs**: Lines coming from left (A, B, C).
  - **Gates**: Gates connected in sequence.
  - **Intermediate Values**: Label wires between gates (P, Q).
  - **Output**: Final line to right (X).
  - **Example**: `(A AND B) OR (NOT C)`.
    - AND gate connected to A, B.
    - NOT gate connected to C.
    - OR gate connected to output of AND and NOT.

## Concept: Logic Expressions
- **Type**: Visual Translation
- **Description**: Converting circuits to text.
- **Prompt**: Create a visual translation for "Logic Expressions".
  - **Circuit**: A simple AND gate feeding into an OR gate.
  - **Text**: `X = (A AND B) OR C`.
  - **Boolean Algebra**: `X = (A . B) + C`.
  - **Annotation**: "Brackets `()` show which operations happen first (like in Math)."
