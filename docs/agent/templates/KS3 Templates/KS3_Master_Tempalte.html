Concepts & Skills

Concept: Data

How the data you create online shapes your digital identity.

Skill: Digital Literacy

Recognising what information becomes part of your digital footprint.

Lesson Objectives

  • Define digital citizenship and digital identity.
  • Explain what a digital footprint is and where it comes from.
  • Identify examples of public vs private data.

The Big Question

"If someone tried to learn about you using only your online Data, what would they find?"

Task: Discuss with your partner.

Core Concept

Digital Footprint

The trail of data you create while using the internet.
